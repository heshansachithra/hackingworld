Introduction to Hacking

Hacking is the practice of modifying the features of a system to accomplish a goal outside of the creator's original purpose. People who engage in hacking activities are often called hackers.

Types of Hackers

Hackers are generally categorized into three types: white hat, black hat, and grey hat. White hat hackers are ethical hackers who help improve security, black hat hackers have malicious intent, and grey hat hackers fall somewhere in between.

Learning to Hack

Becoming a hacker requires knowledge of computer systems, programming languages, and cybersecurity principles. Many resources are available online to help aspiring hackers learn and improve their skills.
